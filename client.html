Welcome to Apartrent FAQ

General Questions

For Rentors

How do I apply to rentals?

You can apply to any Apartrent listing with an "Rent" option.
When you submit an application, it's securely shared with the listing's property owner or manager. Apartrent will let them know you've applied and they can review your application right away.

Property manager is not responding?

Listings on Apartments.com are self-managed by the property manager, owner or agent, and we're sorry we're not able to help here. If a property manager is not answering the phone, returning a call, or responding to an email, you may want to move on to another rental that interests you.

For Rentee

How do I edit a listing?

If you'd like to make changes to your listing, First visit Apartrent website and click on Sign In to log into your account. Now that you are on our dashboard you can select your desired listing to be edited. Click the pen icon to make changes then save.

I'm having issues logging in...

Securing your account is a top priority at Apartrent! To ensure that only you and your associates have access to the listings and account information, we've added Two Factor authentication for added security.
